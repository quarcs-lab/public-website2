This paper proposes a GDP nowcasting strategy for Bolivia that combines machine learning with traditional indicators and satellite imagery (nighttime lights, built-up area, and vegetation and precipitation measures for major crops), narrowing the economic-activity release lag from six to two months. The approach won first place in the 2023 CEMLA Central Bank Award.
