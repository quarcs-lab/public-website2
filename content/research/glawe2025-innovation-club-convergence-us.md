---
title: "Club convergence in innovation activity: Evidence from the US"
author: Linda Glawe
date: "2025-12-01"
type: post
tags: ["Papers in Regional Science"]
---

This paper identifies convergence clubs in innovation activity across US states and uses an ordered regression model to explain club membership, drawing on measures of human capital (college completion for education quantity and grade-8 math scores for quality). It finds a small number of distinct innovation clubs rather than uniform convergence.

[- Link to the published paper](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.pirs.2025.100127)
