---
title: "Testing for convergence clubs in real wage across Indonesian provinces from 2008 to 2020"
author: Muhamad Rifki Maulana and Harry Aginta
date: "2022-01-01"
type: post
tags: ["Regional Statistics"]
---

This study tests for convergence clubs in real wages across Indonesian provinces over the 2008–2020 period using the log t regression approach. Rather than a single national trend, the results identify multiple convergence clubs, revealing persistent regional wage disparities and the factors associated with club membership.
