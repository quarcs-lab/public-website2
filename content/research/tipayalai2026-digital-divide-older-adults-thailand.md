title: "Not Over the Hill: Exploring the Digital Divide Among Vulnerable Older Adults in Thailand"
author: Katikar Tipayalai, Nattasit Chittavimongkhon and Panjapon Sattayanurak
date: "2026-02-18"
type: post
tags: ["Journal of Aging & Social Policy"]
---

Using survey data from Lampang province, this paper measures the digital competence of vulnerable older adults across five domains and finds that income, education, and personal technology access are significant predictors of digital capability, with device ownership and internet connectivity as key barriers to accessing government welfare services.
