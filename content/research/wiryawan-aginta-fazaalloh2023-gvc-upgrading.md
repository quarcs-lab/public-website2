---
title: "Does GVC participation help industrial upgrading in developing countries? New evidence from panel data analysis"
author: Bangkit A. Wiryawan, Harry Aginta and Al Muizzuddin Fazaalloh
date: "2023-01-01"
type: post
tags: ["The Journal of International Trade & Economic Development"]
---

This study examines whether participation in global value chains (GVCs) promotes industrial upgrading in developing countries, using panel data analysis across a sample of developing economies. The results provide new evidence on the conditions under which GVC integration translates into higher domestic value added and industrial upgrading.
