Interactive Data Explorations:

  • Is Indonesia suffering from premature deindustrialization? If so, what are the regional dynamics of this process?

  • How large are the productivity differences across countries in the ASEAN community? Are there signs of convergence? What is the relationship among different measures of productivity?

  • How large are the income differences across subnational regions of the world? What is the relationship between regional inequality and regional income per-capita?

  • Using cross-sectional data, what is the relationship between life expectancy and GDP per capita? How does it differ across continents? Why?

  • Using panel data, what is the relationship between life expectancy and GDP per capita? How does it differ across continents? Can you find something unintuitive in the Americas? Why?

  • What is the relationship between GDP per capita and CO2 emissions per capita? How does it differ across regions? Can you find something unintuitive in South Asia? Why?
